Her story

A trusted advisor, a dedicated partner

Whether you're thinking about your family, home, career, health, or retirement — or any combination of these — Carrie Wang has the expertise and the heart to guide you toward a brighter, more financially secure life.

As the founder of Seven Lakes Financial Services Inc. and a licensed Sun Life advisor serving clients across Ontario and Saskatchewan, Carrie brings years of experience in the financial services industry to every client relationship she builds.

Her approach is personal, collaborative, and always tailored to your unique situation. Carrie will work closely with you to understand your financial goals, prepare for the unexpected, and take advantage of new opportunities — throughout every stage of your life.

Carrie is fluent in English, Mandarin, and Taiwanese, making her uniquely positioned to serve clients from diverse cultural backgrounds with clarity and care.

Her philosophy

The values that guide every conversation

Carrie believes financial planning is not just about numbers — it's about your life, your family, and your future. These principles shape every client relationship she builds.

01

Listen first, advise second

Every client's situation is unique. Carrie takes the time to truly understand your goals, concerns, and life circumstances before recommending any strategy.

02

Clarity over complexity

Financial planning can be overwhelming. Carrie translates complex concepts into clear, actionable steps so you always feel confident and informed about your choices.

03

A plan for life — not just today

Your needs evolve over time. Carrie builds long-term relationships, regularly reviewing and adapting your plan as your life circumstances change and grow.

How Carrie works with you

What to expect when you work with Carrie

Carrie's process is built on trust, transparency, and a genuine commitment to your long-term wellbeing.

1

Discovery conversation

Your journey starts with a relaxed, no-obligation conversation. Carrie listens to understand your life situation, financial goals, family needs, and any concerns you may have. No jargon, no pressure.

2

Financial review & assessment

Carrie reviews your current financial picture — income, assets, protection coverage, and existing plans — to identify strengths and gaps, and to understand where you need the most attention.

3

Personalized strategy

Based on your unique goals, Carrie creates a comprehensive, tailored financial plan — covering retirement, estate, insurance, and investment strategies that work together as a whole.

4

Implementation & ongoing support

Carrie walks alongside you as your plan is put into action — and stays in close contact as your life evolves, regularly reviewing and adjusting your strategy to keep you on track.
