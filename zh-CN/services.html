Retirement planning

A confident retirement doesn't happen by accident — it's the result of thoughtful, early planning. Carrie helps you build a clear roadmap so you can stop working on your terms, with the income you need to live the life you've imagined.

Retirement planning starts with understanding where you are today and where you want to be. Carrie reviews your current savings, income sources, and projected expenses to identify gaps and create a strategy that bridges them.

Key areas Carrie covers:

  • RRSP contribution strategy and optimization — maximizing tax deferrals now for greater income later
  • CPP and OAS timing analysis — when to start benefits for maximum lifetime income
  • Pension review and income projection — understanding what your workplace pension actually provides
  • Retirement income drawdown strategy — structuring withdrawals to minimize tax over your lifetime
  • Integration of TFSA, RRSP, and non-registered assets into a cohesive retirement income plan
  • Longevity and care planning — ensuring your plan accounts for a long, healthy retirement

保障您的收入

Critical illness insurance

A serious illness diagnosis can derail even the best financial plan. Critical illness insurance provides a tax-free lump-sum payment so you can focus entirely on recovery — without worrying about money.

Critical illness insurance pays out a lump-sum benefit if you are diagnosed with one of the covered conditions — such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. You receive the money tax-free, to use however you choose: replacing lost income, covering medical costs, or paying off debt.

Why it matters: 1 in 2 Canadians will be diagnosed with cancer in their lifetime. Without critical illness coverage, a serious diagnosis can force families to drain savings, take on debt, or liquidate investments at the worst possible time.

Covered conditions typically include:

  • Cancer (life-threatening)
  • Heart attack and stroke
  • Coronary artery bypass surgery
  • Kidney failure, organ transplants
  • Multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease
  • And 20+ additional conditions depending on your policy
適合对象： 在職成年人（30至50岁） 自僱人士 Business owners Anyone with a mortgage

Tax-free lump-sum payout
