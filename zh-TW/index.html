About Carrie

真正傾聽您需求的信賴顧問

王羽恬 is a Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU®) and Certified Executor Advisor (CEA), and a proud member of the Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) — recognitions that reflect her dedication to excellence and her clients' success.

As the founder of Seven Lakes Financial Services Inc. and a Sun Life advisor serving clients in Ontario and Saskatchewan, Carrie brings deep expertise in retirement, estate, and business planning with a warm, bilingual approach in English, Mandarin, and Taiwanese.

She believes financial planning is not just about numbers — it's about your life, your family, and your future.
